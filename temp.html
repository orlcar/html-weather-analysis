Max Temperature

The weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude), which is an expected result. However, the data shows that the souther hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This could be caused by the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
